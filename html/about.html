About Me

About Brayden White
I am a freshman at Brigham Young University (BYU) and am currently in my second semeseter. I am from Boise, Idaho but have lived in a variety of places including Portland, Seattle, and Brussels, Belgium. Some of my interest include sports, backpacking, and traveling. I have been in Provo since August of 2021 and have enjoyed my time here, loving the opportunity I have to learn.


Interests

Sports
I enjoy playing many different sports. In high school, I played basketball, baseball, and was on the swim team. I had played these sports since I was very young. Currently, I participate in basketball intramurals at BYU and hope to continue honing my skills through the years.



Backpacking
Backpacking is another one of my passions. I go on a least one multi-day backpacking trip each year. Backpacking challenges me physically and mentally, and it is very rewarding when I reach my goal and get to relax and fish.



Travel
Traveling is one of my favorite things to do. While living in Belgium, I had the opportunity to visit much of Europe. I was able to experience many different cultures and see how different people around the world lived. More recently, I have done some U.S. travel and gone to Hawaii, San Diego, and Arizona.
